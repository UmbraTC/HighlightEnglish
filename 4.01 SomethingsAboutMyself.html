Listen and complete the next interview.



  • How long have you been living here?

  • I've been living in Mexico City for fifteen years. I came here to go to the University. It was hard at the beginning but eventually things started to get better .


  • Have you ever lived in another country?

  • No, I haven't. But I know a lot of people that has. My friend Hugo has been living in Alberta, Canada, for almost a year now. I'm going to visit him later this year.


  • What kind of music are you listening to currently?

  • Well, to be honest I don’t really like Latin music. I'm listening to a lot of Australian Alternative Rock right now. I like to listen to music when I study.


  • What's your favorite way of spending a weekend? What do you like to do?

  • I like to go out with my friends. Maybe to a concert or to get some snacks in a bar.


  • When did you last buy something expensive?

  • Three months ago, actually. I needed some gear for my computer. So I bought it.


  • Have you ever performed in front of an audience?

  • Yes, I have. I used to be the drummer of a rock band. I played with my friends for five years but right now we don’t play anymore.


  • What did you do for your last vacation?

  • I went to my mother’s house and spent the weekend with my family. The rest of the days I was relaxing at home, listening to music and reading.


  • Finally, what is the most important thing for your daily routine?

  • Oh, coffee! I can't get through the day without a cup or two.
