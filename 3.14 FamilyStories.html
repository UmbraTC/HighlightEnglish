Exercises

Complete the sentences. Use the correct forms of the verbs given. Sometimes there is more than one correct answer

  1. I want my children (make) good decisions for themselves.


  2. I don't let my kids (play) computer games before bedtime. It makes them (sleep) badly


  3. I usually make my teenagers (clean up) the kitchen after meals.


  4. I want to get my kids (eat) well. They're always eating junk food.


  5. I usually tell my kids (do) their homework before dinner, but I don't help them (do) it.


  6. I'm always asking them (turn off) the lights in the house.


  7. I have them (shut down) their computers at night.


  8. I'm always telling my kids (be) careful when they go out.


  9. I always have my kids (write) thank-you letters when they get a gift.


  10. We often help our sons (study) for exams. We make up games for them.
