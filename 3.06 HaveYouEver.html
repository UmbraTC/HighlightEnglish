• Ask about experiences using present perfect questions.

Exercises

Complete the conversations with the present perfect.

    1. you ever (hear) of kite-surfing?
    2. Yes, I . But I (never // do) anything like that

    1. you and your friends ever (go) on a big roller coaster?
    2. No, we . I always (hate) roller coasters.

    1. you ever (stay) up all night?
    2. Yes, I . My family (go) camping two years ago,and none of us (sleep) all night.

    1. you (do) anything different last summer?
    2. Yes, I . I (learn) to play African drums. I always (want)to play them. I (never // go) to Africa, though.
